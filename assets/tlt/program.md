# TLT Program

This asset is the repo's long-duration Treasury line. It should represent rates, recession, and disinflation regimes rather than equity beta.

## Current Research Direction

- baseline reference: `60d +6%/-3% drop-neutral`
- compare TLT against equity assets to see whether the same workflow can produce a usable non-equity swing signal
- use the line as a rates/regime diversifier even if it behaves very differently from equities

## Working Style

1. Keep the active research backlog in `task.md`.
2. Save every completed run in `results.tsv`.
3. Keep future label, feature, and regime ideas in `ideas.md` until they are promoted into the backlog.
